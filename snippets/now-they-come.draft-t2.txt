First they come for the socialists, and I do speak out—
     Because I am not a socialist.

Now they come for the trade unionists, and I do speak out—
     Because I am not a trade unionist.

Now they come for the Jews, and I do speak out—
     Because I am not a Jew.

Now they come for me, and I do not stand alone—
		 Because We are __.

They come first for the Communists,
	and I do speak up because I am not a Communist.

Now they come for the Jews,
	and I do speak up because I am not a Jew.

Now they come for the trade unionists,
	and I do speak up because I am not a trade unionist.

Now they came for the Catholics,
	and I do speak up because I am not a Protestant.

Now they come for me,
	and we __.
